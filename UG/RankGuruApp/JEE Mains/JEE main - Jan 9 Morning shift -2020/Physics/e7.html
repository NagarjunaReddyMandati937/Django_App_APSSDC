Given ,



    \(\Delta P=700N/m^2\)



    \(a_A=40cm^2\)



    \(a_B=20cm^2\)



    from the continuity equation



    \(a_AV_A=a_BV_B\)



    \(40V_A=20V_B\)



    \(2V_A=V_B\)



    From Bernoulli's equation



    \(P_A+\dfrac{1}{2} \rho V^2_A=P_B+\dfrac{1}{2} \rho V^2_B\) (vertical height same)



    \(P_A-P_B=\dfrac{1}{2} \rho (V^2_B-V_A^2)\)



    \(\Delta P =\dfrac{1}{2}\times 10^3((2V_A)^2-V^2_A)\)



    \(=\dfrac{1}{2}\times 10^3(4V^2_A-V^2_A)\)



    \(\Delta P=\dfrac{3}{2} V^2_A \times 10^3\)



    \(700=\dfrac{3}{2} V^2_A \times 10^3\)



    \(V^2_A=\dfrac{700\times 2}{3\times 1000}\)



    \(=\dfrac{7}{15}m^2/s^2\)



    \(V_A=\sqrt{\dfrac{7}{15}}m/s\)



    \(=\sqrt{\dfrac{7}{15}}\times 100cm/s\)



    \(Vol^m\) flow rate \(=a_A\times v_A\)



    \(=40\times \sqrt{\dfrac{7}{15}}\times 100cm^3/sec\)



    \(Vol^m\) flow rate \(=2732\,cm^3/s \approx 2720cm^3/s\)



    Option B is Correct.
