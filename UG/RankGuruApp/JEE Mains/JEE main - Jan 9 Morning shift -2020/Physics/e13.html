for Gas \(A\)



    degree of freedom (f) \(=3+2=5\) (for diatomic)



    So, \((C_v)_A=\dfrac{1}{2}\times f\times R=\dfrac{5R}{2}\)



    for Gas \(B\)



    Additional vibrational mode is added



    So, degree of freedom \((f)=3+2+2=7\)



    \((C_v)_B=\dfrac{1}{2}fR=\dfrac{7R}{2}\)



    now, Ratio \(=\dfrac{(C_v)_A}{(C_v)_B}=\dfrac{5R/2}{7R/2}=\dfrac{5}{7}\)
