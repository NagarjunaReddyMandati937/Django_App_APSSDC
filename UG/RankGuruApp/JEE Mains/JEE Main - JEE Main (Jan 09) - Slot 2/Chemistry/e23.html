\(\Delta S= \dfrac{\Delta H _{Transition}}{T}\)



    \(\Delta S =nC\ ln {\dfrac{T_f}{T_i}}=ms\ ln {\dfrac{T_f}{T_i}}\)



    n= no. of moles, m= mass, C=molar heat capacity, s= specic heat capacity



    Phase change path



    \(H_2O(s) \xrightarrow {\Delta S_1} H_2O(l) \xrightarrow {\Delta S_2} H_2O (l) \)



    \(\xrightarrow {\Delta S_3} H_2O(g) \xrightarrow {\Delta S_4} H_2O(g)\)



    \(\Delta s_1=\dfrac{\Delta H_{fusion}}{273}=\dfrac{334}{273}=1.22\)



    \(\Delta s_2= 4.2\ ln \dfrac {363}{273}=1.31\)



    \(\Delta s_3=\dfrac{\Delta H_{vap}}{2491}{373}=6.67\)



    \(\Delta s_3=\dfrac{\Delta H_{vap}}{2491}{373}=6.67\)



    \(\Delta s_4= 2.0\ ln \dfrac{383}{373}=0.05\)



    \(\Delta s_{total}=\Delta s_1 +\Delta s_2 +\Delta s_3 +\Delta s_4\)
