\(\alpha, \beta\) are roots of equation \(x^2+2x+2=0\)



    root\(=\dfrac{-2\pm\sqrt{4-8}}{2}=-1\pm i\)



    \(\Rightarrow \alpha =i-i\) and \(\beta =-(1+i)\)



    \(\Rightarrow \alpha^2=-2i\) and \(\beta^2=2i\)



    \(\Rightarrow \alpha^{14}=(-2i)^7\) and \(\beta^{14}=(2i)^7\)



    \(\Rightarrow \alpha^{14}=-128i^3\) and \(\beta^{14}=128i^3\)



    \(=128i\) and \(=-128i\)



    \(\alpha^{15}=128i(i-1)\) and \(\beta^{15}=128i(i+1)=-128+128i\)



    \(=-128-128i\)



    \(\Rightarrow \alpha^{15}+\beta^{15}\)



    \(=-128-128i-128+128i=-256\)
