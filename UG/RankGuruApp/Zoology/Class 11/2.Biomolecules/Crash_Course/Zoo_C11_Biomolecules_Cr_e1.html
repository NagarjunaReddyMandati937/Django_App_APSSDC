• Nucleic acid is a component molecule made up of a nitrogenous base, a phosphate group and a 5-carbon sugar.

  • If 5-carbon sugar is ribose then the nucleic acid is said to be Ribo nucleic acid or RNA.

  • If 5-carbon sugar is 2-deoxy ribose then the nucleic acid is said to be Deoxy ribo nucleic acid or DNA.

  • So, the correct answer is ' None of the above'.
