• Proteins are the most dynamic biomolecules performing a variety of structural and functional roles.

  • The proteins have a high turn over and constantly old or worn out molecules are broken down and newer molecules are synthesized.

  • Sprinson and Rittenberg (1949) estimated that in man, the average half life of the proteins of whole body is of the order of 80 days.

  • Total body protein synthesis is 400 gram per day, establishing an equilibrium between bodily proteins and circulating plasma proteins.
