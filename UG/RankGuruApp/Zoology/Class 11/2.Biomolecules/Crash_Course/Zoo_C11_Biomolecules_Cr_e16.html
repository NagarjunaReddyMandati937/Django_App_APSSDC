• Disaccharides are class of sugars whose molecules contain two monosaccharide residues.

  • It is formed when two monosaccharide molecules displace a hydroxyl radical from one molecule and a hydrogen from the other and the vacant bonds on the monosaccharides are join the two monomers together.

    • Thus, a glycosidic bond is formed between the two monosaccharide molecules.

  • So, the correct answer is 'Glycosidic bonds'.
