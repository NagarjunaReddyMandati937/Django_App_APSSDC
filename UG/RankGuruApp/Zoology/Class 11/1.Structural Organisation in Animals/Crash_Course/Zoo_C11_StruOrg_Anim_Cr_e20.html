• The Earthworm’s skin is protected by a thin cuticle and kept moist by a slimy mucus.

    • This lets it absorb the oxygen it needs and expel carbon dioxide.

    • If a worm is dried up by the sun, it will die because the exchange of gases can’t take place.

  • Asphyxia is the condition where the body either doesn't get enough oxygen to continue normal function or has too much carbon dioxide to function properly.

    • Without adequate oxygen, nerve cells in the brain begin to die in about 2-4 minutes, and cell death is irreversible.

  • So, the correct option is 'Asphyxia'
