• The clitellum is a thick glandular and nonsegmented section of the body wall in the form of a prominent circular band, present in segments 14-15-16 in the earthworm.

  • Clitellum secretes mucous and albumin which helps in the formation of the cocoon and is used for fertilization of eggs.

  • So,the correct answer is 'Segments 14-15-16' .
