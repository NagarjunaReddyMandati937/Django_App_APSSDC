• Glandular epithelium is a type of striated epithelium found in the glands.

  • It covers the organs associated with secretion.

  • It covers the majority of the glands like a salivary gland.

  • Ciliated epithelium is a type of epithelium.

  • It is found in the respiratory tract and the fallopian tubes.

  • Bronchioles are lined by ciliated columnar and ciliated cuboidal epithelium.

  • The inner wall of the oesophagus is lined by non-keratinized stratified squamous epithelium.

  • Other than oesophagus, it lines the mouth and vagina.

  • Squamous epithelium is a type of epithelium that covers the body cavities such as the peritoneum (abdominal cavity), pleura (thoracic cavity).

  • Thus, the correct answer is 'Columnar epithelium - Peritoneum of body cavity.'
