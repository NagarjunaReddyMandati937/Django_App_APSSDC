• The compound epithelium is made of more than one layer of cells and thus has a limited role in secretion and absorption.

  • Their main function is to provide protection against chemical and mechanical stresses.

  • They cover the dry surface of the skin, the moist surface of the buccal cavity, pharynx, inner lining of ducts of salivary glands and of pancreatic ducts.
