• 1) Polygamous plants are those which have both males, female and bisexual flowers on the same plant.

    • Litchi produces a polygamous flower.

  • 2) Dioecious plants have separate plants with either male flowers or female only.

    • Silene is dioecious.

  • 3) Trioecious have three types of flower on the same or on different plants some of the flowers being staminate, others pistillate, and others both.

    • Mango is trioecious.

  • 4) Monoecious plants have both male and female reproductive organs in the same flower.

    • Chara is monoecious.
