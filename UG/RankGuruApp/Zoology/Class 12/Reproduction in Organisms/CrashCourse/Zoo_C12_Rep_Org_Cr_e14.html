• In spermatogenesis, 52 sperm can be produced in 13 meiotic divisions.

  • However, 50 eggs will require 50 meiotic divisions, because one meiotic division in oogenesis produces a single egg.

  • Use the formula x + x/4, where x is the number of seeds.

  • Therefore 50 + 50/4 = 50+ 12.5 = 62.5 = approx 63 divisions.

    • So, the correct answer is '63'.
