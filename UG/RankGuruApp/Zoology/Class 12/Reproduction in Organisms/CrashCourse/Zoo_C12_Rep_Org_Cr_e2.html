• Asexual reproduction is a means of reproduction wherein there is an involvement of one parent only.

  • It does not involve the process of meiosis and no gametes are formed.

  • In plants, the asexual reproduction occurs by means of the vegetative parts like roots, stem and leaves and it is called vegetative propagation in plants.

  • A. Bacteria reproduce asexually by means of binary fission.

  • B. Hydra reproduces asexually by means of budding.

  • C. Strawberries show vegetative propagation by means of runners.

    • Hence, the correct answer is 'All of the above'.
