• While observing permanent slide of Amoeba, Ramesh saw oval shaped nucleus.

  • This nucleus indicates binary fission.

  • Binary fission is a type of asexual reproduction seen in bacteria and amoeba.

  • In this method, the content of cell is replicated and then cell is divided into 2 equal halves forming 2 daughter cells.

  • Before constriction from the center of the cell, the nucleus becomes oval shaped.

  • The oval shaped nucleus indicates dividing nucleus.

    • Thus, the correct answer is 'Binary fission'.
