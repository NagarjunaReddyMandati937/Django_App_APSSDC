• Ectopic pregnancy is a complication of pregnancy, in which the embryo attaches itself outside the uterus.

  • Signs and symptoms of ectopic pregnancy include increased hCG, vaginal bleeding, sudden lower abdominal pain, pelvic pain, a tender cervix.

  • The causes of it can be pelvic inflammatory disease, infertility, use of IUD, tubal surgery, previous history of ectopic pregnancies, smoking and many more.
