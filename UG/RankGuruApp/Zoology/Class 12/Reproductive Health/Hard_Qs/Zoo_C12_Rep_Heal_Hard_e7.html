• Abortion is a phenomenon of the end of pregnancy.

    • It can be either spontaneous or induced.

    • The induced abortion is called a medical termination of pregnancy (MTP).

  • MTP is a medical way of ending an unwanted pregnancy.

    • In this, a qualified gynecologist removes the embryo or fetus from the womb.

    • Vacuum or suction aspiration can be done for MTP.

  • In this, aspiration is done in order to remove uterine contents through the cervix.

    • Abortion can also be induced using mifepristone or RU-486.

    • Since it is given orally, it is a non-surgical way of MTP.

    • Thus, the correct answer is 'RU 486 (mifepristone)'.
