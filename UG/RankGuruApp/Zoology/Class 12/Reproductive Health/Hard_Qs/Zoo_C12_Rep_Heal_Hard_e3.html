• Non-invasive refers to the medical procedures, where there is no use of instruments in the body.

    • Now, Chorionic villi sampling is a test, made in early pregnancy to detect congenital abnormalities in the fetus, in which a villi tissue is removed from the villi of the chorion.

  • Amniocentesis is a process, where amniotic fluid is sampled using a hollow needle inserted into the uterus.

    • This is done to test the health of the fetus, by checking for any type of chromosomal or genetic abnormalities.

    • On the other hand, Foetoscopy is an endoscopic procedure pregnancy to allow surgical access to the fetus, the amniotic cavity, the umbilical cord, and the fetal side of the placenta.

    • Therefore, none of these are non-invasive techniques.
