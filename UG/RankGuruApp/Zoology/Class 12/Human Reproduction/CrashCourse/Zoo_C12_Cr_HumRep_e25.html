• Paramesonephric ducts or Mullerian ducts are paired ducts of the embryo that run down the lateral sides of the urogenital ridge and terminate at the sinus tubercle in the primitive urogenital sinus.

  • In the female, they will develop to form the fallopian tubes, uterus, cervix, and the upper two-thirds of the vagina.

  • The Mullerian duct is formed it differentiates into a functional oviduct, uterus, cervix and upper vagina.
