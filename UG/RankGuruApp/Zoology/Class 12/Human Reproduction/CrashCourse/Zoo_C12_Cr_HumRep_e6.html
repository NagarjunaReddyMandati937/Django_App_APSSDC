• Antrum is the cavity present within the body.

  • There are various antrum present within the body.

  • Follicular antrum is a fluid-filled cavity in the ovaries.

  • It also contains an immature egg cell.

  • The presence of follicular antrum is the sign that primary follicle has matured into a secondary follicle.

  • A secondary follicle contains antrum.

  • Once, the secondary follicle survives, the tertiary follicle is formed.

  • The identifying characteristic of it is antrum.

    • Thus, the correct answer is 'A- Antrum, B- Secondary follicle, C-Tertiary follicle'.
