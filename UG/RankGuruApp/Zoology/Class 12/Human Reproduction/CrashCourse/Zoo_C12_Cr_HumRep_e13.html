• The semen produced by males s made up of secretions from the seminal vesicles, prostate glands, and the bulbourethral glands.

  • Secretions of the seminal vesicles include proteins, enzymes, fructose, mucus, vitamin C, flavins, phosphorylcholine, and prostaglandins.

  • Secretions of the prostate glands include citric acid, acid phosphatase, calcium, sodium, zinc, potassium, and protein-splitting enzymes.

  • And those of bulbourethral glands include mucus.

    • So, the correct answer is 'Citric acid'.
