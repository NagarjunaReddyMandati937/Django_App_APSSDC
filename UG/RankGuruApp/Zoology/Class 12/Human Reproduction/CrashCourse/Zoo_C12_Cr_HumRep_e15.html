• There are 3 germ layers, namely ectoderm, mesoderm and endoderm.

  • These germ layers later give rise to different parts of the body.

  • Ectoderm gives rise to skin, brain and nervous system.

  • Mesoderm gives rise to bones, muscles, heart, circulatory system, internal sex organs.

  • Endoderm gives rise to inner lining of systems, liver, pancreas.

    • Thus, the correct answer is 'Mesoderm'.
