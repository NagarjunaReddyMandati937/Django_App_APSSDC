• Epididymis is a long and a coiled tube that receives sperms from the vasa efferentia.

  • It leads to the vas deferens.

  • It is responsible for storing and nourishing the sperms until they are moved further to the vas deferens.

  • Thereafter, the secretions of the prostate gland and the seminal vesicles will provide nourishment to the sperms.

    • So, the correct answer is 'Epididymis'.
