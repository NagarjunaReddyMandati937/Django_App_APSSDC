• Primary oocyte is a result of the mitosis division of an oocyte, which is surrounded by a membrane known as Zona pellucida, which in turn is surrounded by multi-layered granulosa cells which secrete sex hormones.

  • The granulosa cells, in turn, are enclosed in a thin layer of extracellular matrix or basal lamina.

  • Outside the basal lamina, the layer Theca interna and Theca externa are found.
