• Vas deferens is a fibromuscular tube that is continuous with epididymis.

  • Sperms are stored in the proximal portion of Vas deferens, near epididymis.

  • While epididymis is an organ where storage and maturation of sperm occurs.

  • Then the sperms are propelled by peristalsis through the tubes and become motile.

  • So upon ejaculation, the epididymis contracts and expels sperm into vas deferens.

    • So, the correct answer is 'Vas deferens'.
