• Menopause is the time when menstrual cycles permanently cease.

  • Symptoms of menopause include irregular periods, hot flashes, vaginal dryness, mood swings.

  • During natural menopause, estrogen and progesterone levels in blood decreases.

  • However, the levels of FSH and LH in blood increases during this time.

  • High levels of FSH is an indicator of perimenopause period.
