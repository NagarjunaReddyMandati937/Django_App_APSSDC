• The male reproductive system is located in the pelvic region.

  • It includes a pair of testes along with accessory ducts, glands and the external genitalia.

  • The accessory ducts include rete testis, vasa efferentia, epididymis and vas deferens.

  • The seminiferous tubules of the testis open into the vasa efferentia through the rete testis.

  • The vasa efferentia leaves the testis and open into epididymis located along the posterior surface of each testis.

  • The epididymis leads to vas deferens that ascend to the abdomen and loops over the urinary bladder.

    • So, the correct option is ' Seminiferous tubules of testis open into the rete testis'.
