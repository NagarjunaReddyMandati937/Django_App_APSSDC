• Female anopheles mosquito is the primary host for Plasmodium species since the parasite completes its sexual cycle in the gut of mosquito.

  • The gametocytes formed in the human blood reach the mosquito gut when the mosquito feeds on human blood.

  • These gametocytes further develop into gametes, undergo fertilization and complete their sexual cycle in the mosquito gut.

    • So, the correct answer is, 'The gut of mosquito'.
