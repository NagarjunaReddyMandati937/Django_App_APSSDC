• A scout worker honey bee performs a waggle dance to convey the information to the rest of the hive about the food source.

  • The direction the bee moves in relation to the hive.

  • This indicates the direction of food with respect to direction of sun.

  • If it moves vertically and upward, the source is directly towards the Sun.

  • If it moves vertically and downward, the source of food is in opposite direction of sun.

  • The duration of the tail wagging dance signifies the distance.

    • Thus, the correct answer is 'Tail wagging dance - downward'.
