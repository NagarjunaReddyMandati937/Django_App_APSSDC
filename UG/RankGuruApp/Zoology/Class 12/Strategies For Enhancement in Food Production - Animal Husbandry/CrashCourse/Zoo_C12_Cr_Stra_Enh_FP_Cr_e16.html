• Basically, germplasm is genetic material of the germ cells.

  • Any living tissue from which new plants can be grown, is included under germplasm which may be a seed, leaves, pollen etc.

  • Germplasm is meant for the conservation of living genetic resources for plant breeding.

  • Hence, only improved and superior varieties of crops are included.

    • So, the correct answer is 'Only improved varieties of crop'.
