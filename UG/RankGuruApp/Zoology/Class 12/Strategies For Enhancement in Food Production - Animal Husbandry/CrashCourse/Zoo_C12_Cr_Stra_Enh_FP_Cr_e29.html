• A- Single-cell proteins (SCP) are produced by a range of microorganisms and substrate.

  • They are dried cells of microorganism.

  • When SCP is produced, the microbial products produce sufficient proteinaceous substances that can be utilized as protein supplements for animal feeds or human foods.

  • It is estimated that algae grown in ponds can produce 20 tons (dry weight) of protein, per acre, per year.
