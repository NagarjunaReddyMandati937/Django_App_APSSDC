• A line consists of a group of individual related by descent and often similar in genotype.

  • The individual lines used in hybridization is known as parents.

  • Hybridization is the process of mating two lines differing in their genotype.

  • An offspring hence produced is called as hybrid.

  • Emasculation is the process in which the stamen of the flower is removed before the dehiscence of anthers.

  • This is done to prevent self hybridization.
