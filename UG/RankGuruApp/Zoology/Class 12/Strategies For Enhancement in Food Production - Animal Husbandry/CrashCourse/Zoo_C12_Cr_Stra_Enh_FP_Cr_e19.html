• Totipotency is the ability of a single cell to divide and produce all of the differentiated cells in an organism.

  • Spores and zygotes are examples of totipotent cells.

  • In the spectrum of cell potency, totipotency represents the cell with the greatest differentiation potential because of totipotency we can grow flower, fruit or an organism from a cell in a culture medium.
