• Waggle dance or wagtail dance is a kind of dance that is performed by honey bees to share the information of food sources located at distance in the range of 50 - 100 km.

  • Waggle dance depends upon the angle of the sun with the food source.

  • Honey bees make different waggle movements according to the location of food and its angle with the sun.

  • If the food source is in direction of the sun, the honey bee will waggle in a vertical direction in such a way that it will move opposite to upright down waggle movement.
