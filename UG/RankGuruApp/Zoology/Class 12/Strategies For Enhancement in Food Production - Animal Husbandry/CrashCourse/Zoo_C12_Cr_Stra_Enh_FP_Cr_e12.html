• Single cell protein means the protein which can be obtained by the culture of single-celled organisms and these proteins are generally used as dietary supplements.

  • Bacteria, algae, fungus all of them are either single-celled structures or the groups have one or more forms which are single-celled.

  • Thus, all of them form sources of single-celled protein.

    • So, the correct answer is 'All of the above'.
