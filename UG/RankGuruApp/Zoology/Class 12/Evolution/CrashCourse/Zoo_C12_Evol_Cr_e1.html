Various mineral elements present in living organisms include
    (i) major elements, and they are:-

  • Carbon (C), nitrogen (N), oxygen (O), hydrogen (H), phosphorus (P), calcium (Ca), magnesium (Mg), sodium (Na), potassium (K) and chlorine (Cl) and

    • (ii) trace elements, and they are:-

  • Iodine (I), iron (Fe), manganese (Mn), cobalt (Co), copper (Cu), zinc (Zn), molybdenum (Mo), boron etc.
