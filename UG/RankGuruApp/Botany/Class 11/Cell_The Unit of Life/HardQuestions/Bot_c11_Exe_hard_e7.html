• Cell theory was proposed by a German botanist, Matthias Schleiden and English zoologist, Theodor Schwann in the year 1839.

  • Cell theory was considered as the most acceptable theory which, elucidated the functioning of a cell.

    The cell theory includes the following points -

  • All living things are made up of cells

  • The cell is the smallest living thing that can perform all the functions of life.

  • Later on, in the year 1855 Rudolf Virchow added a new point in cell theory that all cells must come from pre-existing cells, which was also known as "Omnis cellula e cellula".
