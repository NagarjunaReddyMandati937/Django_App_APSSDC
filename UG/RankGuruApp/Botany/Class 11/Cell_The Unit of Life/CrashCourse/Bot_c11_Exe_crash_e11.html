Cells are basically of two types :

  • prokaryotic and eukaryotic cell.

  • All bacteria are prokaryotes.

  • All protists, fungi, plants and animals have eukaryotic cells.

  • The prokaryotic cells are small in size (typically 0.2 to 2 μm).

  • The eukaryotic cells are comparatively larger in size (typically 10 to 100 μm).
