• It is given that the spore of bryophytes contains 5 chromosomes and 5 picograms of DNA.

  • Spore is haploid in nature.

  • Spore mother cell is diploid in nature.

  • Due to this, the spore mother cells will have double amount of chromosome and DNA.

  • During G2 phase, the amount of DNA remains the same and DNA doubles.

  • In a spore mother cell of bryophytes, the chromosome number will be 10 (2n).

  • However, amount of DNA will be double, that is 20 (2n) picograms.

    • Thus, the correct answer is '10 chromosome, 20 pg DNA.
