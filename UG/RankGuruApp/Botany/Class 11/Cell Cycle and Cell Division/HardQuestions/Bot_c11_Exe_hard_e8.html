• Prophase is the first phase of mitosis, that separates the duplicates genetic material carried in the nucleus of the parent cell into two identical daughter cells.

  • In metaphase, the spindle fibres are formed and get attached to the centromere of each chromosome.

  • In Anaphase the chromosomes move towards the opposite poles due to contraction of spindle fibre.

  • In telophase, daughter nuclei are formed.

    • So, the correct option is 'Metaphase'.
