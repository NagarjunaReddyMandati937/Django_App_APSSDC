During the G2 phase, the organelles are duplicated and the proteins required for the division are synthesized.

  • A. During M-phase the karyokinesis and cytokinesis occur. During the karyokinesis, the division of chromosomes occurs. After that, the cytoplasm is divided in cytokinesis.

  • B. During G1 phase the machinery of proteins required for DNA replication occurs.

  • C. No division activity occurs during the G0 phase, It is called the quiescent phase or resting phase.

  • D. During the G2 phase, organelles are duplicated and the cell commits for the division.

    • Hence, the correct answer is 'G2 phase'.
