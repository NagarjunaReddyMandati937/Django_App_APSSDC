• A cell is divided into two identical cells in mitosis. Stage prior to this is interphase.

  • During interphase, the cell grows, accumulating all the required nutrients, preparing to cell division and duplicate its DNA.

  • In the mitotic phase, karyokinesis in which cell's chromosomes are divided and cytokinesis in which cytoplasm divides to form two daughter cells takes place.

  • After completing cytokinesis, the cell enters the G1 stage of interphase.

  • Then it goes through S and G2 stages before starting mitosis again.This cycle repeats.

    • So the correct answer is the G1 phase.
