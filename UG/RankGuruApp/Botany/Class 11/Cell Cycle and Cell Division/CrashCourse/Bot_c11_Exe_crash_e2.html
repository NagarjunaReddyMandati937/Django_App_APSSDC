• G1 phase is also known as first growth phase or post mitotic gap phase.

  • It is the phase between end of mitotic phase of previous cell and initiation of S-phase of next mitotic phase.

  • During G1 phase the cells is metabolically active and continuously grows.

  • In this phase different type of RNA (mRNA,tRNA and rRNA) and proteins are synthesised.
