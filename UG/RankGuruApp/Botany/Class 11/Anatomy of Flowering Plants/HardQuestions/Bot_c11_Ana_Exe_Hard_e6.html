• Medullary rays are the vertical plates of parenchyma cells running parallel through the cylinder of vascular tissues.

  • Its functions are:-

  • Radial conduction of water, minerals, and other organic substances.

  • Food storage.

  • Deposition of tannins, crystals.

  • Gaseous exchange through the intercellular spaces.

  • Helps in secondary growth.

  • Interfascicular cambium origin.

    • So the correct option is 'All of the above are correct'.
