• The apical meristems are situated at the apex of roots and shoot.

  • They are responsible for growth in length in dicots and monocots.

  • It is known as primary growth.

  • The lateral meristematic tissues are cambium and cork cambium.

  • The lateral meristems tissues are responsible for the increase in girth of the plant by secondary growth.

  • So, the correct answer is 'Lateral meristem'.
