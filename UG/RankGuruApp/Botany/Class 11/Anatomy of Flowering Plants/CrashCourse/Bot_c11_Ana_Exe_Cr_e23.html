• Springwood is formed due to the rapid activity of the vascular cambium.

  • The vessels are wider in lumen.

  • It is light and has low density.

  • Heartwood is the wood that has non-functional vessels due to deposition of lignin by tyloses.

  • It appears dark and has more density.

    • So, the correct answer is springwood is lighter in colour and has a lower density as compared to heartwood.
