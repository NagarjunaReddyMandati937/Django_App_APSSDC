• Xylem and phloem together called Vascular bundles.

  • In the vascular bundles cambium is present between the xylem and phloem are called Open vascular bundles.

  • These are found in Dicotyledonous stems. eg:Brassica, Capsicum, Nicotiana, Cicer, Petunia, Sesbania(6).

  • Vascular bundles in which cambium is not present between the xylem and phloem are called closed vascular bundles.

  • These are found in monocotyledonous stems. eg:Tulipa, Gloriosa, Aloe, Asparagus, Colchicum, Zea(6).
