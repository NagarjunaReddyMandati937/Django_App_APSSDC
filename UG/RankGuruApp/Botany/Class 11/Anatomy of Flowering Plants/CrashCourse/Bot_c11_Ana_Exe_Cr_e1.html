• The first formed primary xylem elements are called protoxylem and the later formed primary xylem is called metaxylem.

  • In stems, the protoxylem lies towards the centre (pith) and the metaxylem lies towards the periphery of the organ.

  • The increase of metaxylem vessels takes place in correspondence with the increase in the total teal area of plant.
