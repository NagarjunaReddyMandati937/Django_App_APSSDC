• Pith or medulla is a tissue in the stems of vascular plants.

  • Pith is composed of soft, spongy parenchyma cells, which store and transport nutrients throughout the plant stem.

  • In monocot stem, it extends also into flowering stems and roots.

  • The pith is the centremost tissue in a dicot stem which is encircled by a ring of xylem.

  • The xylem, in turn, is encircled by a ring of phloem.

    • So, The correct answer is 'Pith'.
