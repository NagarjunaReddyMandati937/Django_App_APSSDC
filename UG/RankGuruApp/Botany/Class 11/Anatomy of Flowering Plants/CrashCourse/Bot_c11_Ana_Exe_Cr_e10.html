• In dicot stem, the lateral branches are exogenous in origin and they come out from the cortex of the stem.

    • However, in dicot root, the lateral roots are endogenous in origin because they come out from the pericycle of the stem.

  • A. Pericycle gives rise to the lateral roots.

  • B. Cortex gives rise to the lateral branches in stem.

  • C. Pith is a storage organ.

  • D. Epidermis gives rise to scales and trichomes.

    • Hence, the correct answer is 'Cortex'.
