• A) Exarch condition of xylem means, the protoxylem lies towards the center and the metaxylem lies towards the periphery. It is found in roots.

  • B) An endarch condition of xylem means, the metaxylem lies toward the center and the protoxylem lies towards the periphery. It is found in the stem.

    • So the correct option is 'Exarch, Endarch'.
