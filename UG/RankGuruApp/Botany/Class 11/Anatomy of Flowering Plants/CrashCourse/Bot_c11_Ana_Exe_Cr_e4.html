• Iodine is used to test the presence of starch in the test material.

  • It binds with starch to give a blue colour.

  • The endodermis is the stem of dicot plants is made up of parenchymatous cells and it stores the starch.

  • Therefore, it is also called the starch sheath.

  • When stained with iodine, the endodermis will take up the blue colour due to the presence of starch.

    • Hence, The correct answer is 'Endodermis'.
