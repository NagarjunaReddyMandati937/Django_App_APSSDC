• The ground tissue of plants includes all tissues that are neither dermal nor vascular.

  • It can be divided into three types parenchyma, collenchyma, and sclerenchyma.

  • Cortex is composed of thin-walled parenchymal cells.

  • The pericycle is a cylinder of parenchyma or sclerenchyma cells that lies just inside the endodermis and is the outer most part of the style of plants.

    • So, the correct answer is 'Cortex and pericycle'.
