• In a woody dicot stem, the arrangement of tissues from the outer side to the inner side is phellem-secondary cortex-secondary phloem-autumn wood.

  • Phellem is the cork tissue made on the outermost side by the phellogen.

  • Secondary cortex is on the inner side by the phellogen.

  • Secondary phloem is produced towards the outer side by the vascular cambium and autumn wood or the secondary xylem is produced towards the inner side by the vascular cambium.

    • Hence, the correct answer is 'IV, I, III'.
