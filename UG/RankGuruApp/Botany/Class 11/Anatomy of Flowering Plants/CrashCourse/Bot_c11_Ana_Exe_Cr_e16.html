• Meristem is the type of tissue that contains undifferentiated cells.

  • They give rise to different organs of a plant.

  • They are present in zones where growth takes place.

  • There are two types of meristems, primary and secondary meristems.

  • The primary meristem is responsible for the production of vascular cambium, cork cambium, and secondary tissues.

  • Procambium, a type of primary meristem give rise to secondary meristematic tissue.

  • The secondary meristem is responsible for the production of permanent tissues.

    • Thus, the correct answer is 'Primary meristem'.
