• Stamen is the unit of androecium.

    • Stamens collectively form the androecium.

  • Microsporophyll bears microsporangia which contains numerous pollen grains.

    • Microsporophyll is modified into stamens.

    • Hence, stamen is also known as microsporophyll.

  • The stamen consists of a stalk called filament and an anther which bears microsporangia.

  • Stamens are the male sex organ of the flower because it transfers pollen grains from anther to pistil of the flower.
