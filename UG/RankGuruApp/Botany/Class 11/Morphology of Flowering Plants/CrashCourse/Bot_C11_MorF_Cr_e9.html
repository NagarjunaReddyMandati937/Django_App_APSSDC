• Adventitious roots are those parts which arise from the organ other than the roots.

  • They usually arise from the stem, internodes, leaves.

  • They may be underground or aerial.

  • Examples include rhizomes, corms, tubers.

    • A. The reproductive root of Asparagus is an adventitious root. It occurs in clusters and it is swollen.

    • B. In sweet potato, adventitious roots grow from the nodes of the running stem. They are also called as storage roots and edible root-tubers.

    • C. Conical roots of carrot is an example of tap root.

    • D. In banyan tree, horizontal aerial branches give rise to aerial adventitious roots. They are also called as prop roots as they support horizontal branches.

    • Thus, the correct answer is 'Conical roots of carrot'.
