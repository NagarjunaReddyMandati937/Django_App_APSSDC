  • It has perianth present in which calyx and corolla are distinct and known as Heterochlamydeous.

  • Sepals are free that is polysepalous.

  • Petals are fused with each other.

  • They are gamosepalous.

  • Four anthers are present and are tetramerous.

  • The flower is bisexual means both pistil and stamens are present.
