The sequences of events occur in a fungal sexual cycle

  • Plasmogamy- In plasmogamy, the cytoplasm of two cells fuses together without the fusion of nuclei. This process helps to bring the haploid nuclei in the same cell.

  • Karyogamy- It is the fusion of the nuclei. Karyogamy results in the formation of a zygote which is diploid.

  • Meiosis- After the formation of zygote, meiosis (reduction division) starts. After meiosis haploid stage of fungi gets established.

  • Thus the correct sequence of events is plasmogamy, karyogamy, and meiosis.
