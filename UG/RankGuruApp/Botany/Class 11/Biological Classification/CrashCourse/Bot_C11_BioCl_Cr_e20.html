• It is given that, the bacteria undergoes binary fission in every minute.

  • It means it has doubling time of 1 minute.

  • It doubles in every minute.

  • The bacteria can fill up a cup in 1 hour.

  • Hence, in 59 minutes, the cup will be half filled.

  • So, during the 60 minute, the bacteria will double and the cup will be full filled from half filled.

    • Thus, the correct answer '59 minutes.'
