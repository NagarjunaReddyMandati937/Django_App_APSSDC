• Prokaryotic cells lack membrane bound organelles and the process of respiration occurs in an area known as the mesosome within the cytoplasm.

    • The mesosome is created as the plasma membrane folds inward, and within this region are respiratory enzymes which function within an electron transport chain.

  • Thus Clostridium is the only prokaryote among the other three options given.

    • Chlorella, Chara and Chlamydomonas are algae and hence are eukaryotes.

    • So, the correct option is ' Clostridium'
