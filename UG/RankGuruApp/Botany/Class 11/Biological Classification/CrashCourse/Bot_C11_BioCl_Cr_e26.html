• Chlorophyta is the green algae that contain the pigment chlorophyll A and chlorophyll b in the well-defined chloroplast.

  • Plants are considered to have evolved from Chlorophyta because plants are green in color and they have chlorophyll A and chlorophyll b pigment just like the Chlorophyta family.

  • There is well-defined chloroplast which stores this pigment in plants.

    • So, the correct option is 'chlorophytes, because of similarities in chloroplasts and accessory pigments'.
