• Basidiomycetes are the most advanced fungi.

    • The basidiocarp or the fruiting body is a large sporophore in which sexually produced spores are formed on a club-shaped basidium.

    • Karyogamy and meiosis occur in the club-shaped basidium.

    • A basidium produces four exogenous meiospores or basidiospores.

  • Thus, from the above discussion, it is clear that basidiospores are exogenously formed.

    • So, the correct answer for the odd statement is 'Basidiospores are endogenously produced on basidium'.
