• Dinoflagellates or unicellular eukaryotic organisms are with two whip-like tails called flagella used for movement.

  • They can be found in marine and freshwater environments all over the world.

  • Hence, A slide under a microscope shows the following features :
    • i. Unicellularity
    • ii. Well defined nucleus
    • iii. Biflagellate. Identifies as Dinoflagellate'

    • So, the correct answer is 'Dinoflagellate'.
