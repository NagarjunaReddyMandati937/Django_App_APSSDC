• Lichen is a symbiotic partnership between fungus and alga.

  • It has algal and fungal partners in its thallus.

  • In this, the fungus is a dominant partner.

  • Hence, lichen has majority of characteristics of fungus.

  • It absorbs sulphur dioxide from the atmosphere.

  • Hence, it is widely used as indicator of air pollution.

  • It grows very slowly.

  • It is eaten by snails, squirrels and some species of monkeys and reindeers during winter.
