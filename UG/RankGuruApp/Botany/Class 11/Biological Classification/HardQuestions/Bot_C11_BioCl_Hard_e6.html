• Saprophytic fungus are those fungus which obtain their nutrients from the dead organic matter.

  • Mould is one of the common saprophyte.

  • It is also called as mucor.

  • It relies on dead organic matter rich in carbohydrates.

  • Yeast also commonly grows on sugary matters.

  • It is seen in nectaries of flowers, juices, etc.

  • Other examples of saprophytic fungus includes Rhizopus, Aspergillus, Penicillium, Mushroom.

  • Smut and rust fungi are facultative saprophytes.

  • Mainly they are parasitic in nature.

  • However they are saprophytes under certain conditions.
