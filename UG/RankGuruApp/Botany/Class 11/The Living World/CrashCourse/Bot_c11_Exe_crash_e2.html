• The suffix 'aceae' is used for the family while 'ales' suffix is used for the order. Rice and brinjal belong to different families as well as order.

  • The suffix 'phyta' indicates the sub-kingdom level. Rice and brinjal belong to the same subkingdom i.e. Tracheophyta.

  • While 'phyceae' suffix is used to denote classes of algae.

    • So, the correct option is 'phyta'
