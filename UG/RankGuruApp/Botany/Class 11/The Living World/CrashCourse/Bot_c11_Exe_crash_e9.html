Artificial classification is based on one or two morphological characters.

  • Cytotaxonomy- It is the use of cytological characters of plants in classification or in solving taxonomical problems.

  • Karyotaxonomy- It is based on the interrelationships of individuals due to chromosomal similarities.

  • The natural system of classification- In this type of classification plants are classified on the basis of their complete morphological characteristics.
