    \[ \Rightarrow p=\cfrac{x+y\cfrac{dy}{dx}}{1+\cfrac{dy}{dx}}=\cfrac{xdx+ydy} {dx+dy} \]

    \[ \Rightarrow (xdy-ydy)^{2}+(ydx-xdx)^{2}\]
