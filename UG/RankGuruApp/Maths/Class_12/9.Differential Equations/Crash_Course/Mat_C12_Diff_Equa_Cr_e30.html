\[ \Rightarrow y\dfrac {dy} {dx} =\dfrac {2y^{2}}{x}-x \]


    Let

    \[ y^{2}=t\Rightarrow 2y\dfrac {dy} {dx} =\dfrac {dt} {dx} \]


    \[ \Rightarrow \dfrac {1}{2}\dfrac {dt} {dx} =\dfrac {2t}{x}-x \]


    \[ \Rightarrow \dfrac {dt} {dx} - 4\dfrac {t} {x}= - 2x \]


    Integrating factor =

    \[ e^{\int \dfrac {-4}{x}dx}=e^{-4lnx}=\dfrac {1}{x^{4}} \]


    Solution

    \[ t. \dfrac {1}{x^{4}}=\int \dfrac {1}{x^{4}}.-2xdx \]


    \[ \Rightarrow y^{2}. \dfrac {1}{x^{4}}=\dfrac {1}{x^{2}}+c \]


    \[ \Rightarrow y^{2}=x^{2}+cx^{4} \]
