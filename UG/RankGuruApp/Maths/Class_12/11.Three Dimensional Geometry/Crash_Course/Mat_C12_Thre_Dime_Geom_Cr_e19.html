Consider the problem


    Let the given lines be


    \[ r=a_1+\lambda b_2 \]


    And

    \[ r=a_2+\lambda b_2 \]


    Now, Shortest distance between two lines


    \[ d = \left| {\dfrac{{\left( {{{\vec a}_2} - {{\vec a}_1}} \right).\left( {{{\vec b}_1} \times {{\vec b}_2}} \right)}}{{\left| {{{\vec b}_1} \times {{\vec b}_2}} \right|}}} \right| \]

    \[ a_2-a_1=-4\bar i+4\bar j-6\bar k \]
