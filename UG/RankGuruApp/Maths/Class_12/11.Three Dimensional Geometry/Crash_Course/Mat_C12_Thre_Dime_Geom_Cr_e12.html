    \[ AB\times BC=-i-7j-4k \]


    so plane

    \[ x+7y+4z=c \]

    will contain

    \[ C(1,1,3) \]

    so it will satisfy plane'equation

    \[ 1+7(1)+4(3)=c \]


    \[ c=20 \]


    Hence equation of plane will be

    \[ x+7y+4z=20 \]

    \[ (5,7,8) \]
