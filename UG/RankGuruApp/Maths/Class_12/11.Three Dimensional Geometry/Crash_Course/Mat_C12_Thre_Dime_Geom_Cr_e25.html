Let

    \[ \overrightarrow{r}=l\left(\overrightarrow{b}\times\overrightarrow{c}\right)+m\left(\overrightarrow{c}\times\overrightarrow{a}\right)\]


    \[+n\left(\overrightarrow{a}\times \overrightarrow{b}\right) \]


    \[ \overrightarrow{r}.\overrightarrow{a}=l\left(\overrightarrow{b}\times \overrightarrow{c}\right)\overrightarrow{a}+m\left(\overrightarrow{c}\times \overrightarrow{a}\right)\overrightarrow{a}\]


    \[+n\left(\overrightarrow{a}\times \overrightarrow{b}\right)\overrightarrow{a} \]


    \[ \overrightarrow{r}.\overrightarrow{a}=l\left(\overrightarrow{b}\times \overrightarrow{c}\right)\overrightarrow{a}+0+0 \]


    since cross product of like vectors

    \[ =0 \]


    \[ \Rightarrow 1=l\left[\overrightarrow{a},\overrightarrow{b},\overrightarrow{c}\right] \]


    \[ \Rightarrow l=1 \]


    \[ \overrightarrow{r}.\overrightarrow{b}=l\left(\overrightarrow{b}\times \overrightarrow{c}\right)\overrightarrow{b}+m\left(\overrightarrow{c}\times \overrightarrow{a}\right)\overrightarrow{b}\]


    \[+n\left(\overrightarrow{a}\times \overrightarrow{b}\right)\overrightarrow{b} \]


    \[ \overrightarrow{r}.\overrightarrow{b}=0+m\left(\overrightarrow{c}\times \overrightarrow{a}\right)\overrightarrow{b}+0 \]


    since cross product of like vectors

    \[ =0 \]


    \[ \Rightarrow 2=m\left[\overrightarrow{a},\overrightarrow{b},\overrightarrow{c}\right] \]


    \[ \Rightarrow m=2 \]


    \[ \overrightarrow{r}.\overrightarrow{c}=l\left(\overrightarrow{b}\times \overrightarrow{c}\right)\overrightarrow{c}+m\left(\overrightarrow{c}\times \overrightarrow{a}\right)\overrightarrow{c}\]


    \[+n\left(\overrightarrow{a}\times \overrightarrow{b}\right)\overrightarrow{c} \]


    \[ \overrightarrow{r}.\overrightarrow{c}=0+0+n\left(\overrightarrow{a}\times \overrightarrow{b}\right)\overrightarrow{c} \]


    since cross product of like vectors

    \[ =0 \]


    \[ \Rightarrow 3=n\left[\overrightarrow{a},\overrightarrow{b},\overrightarrow{c}\right] \]


    \[ \Rightarrow n=3 \]


    \[ \therefore \overrightarrow{r}=\overrightarrow{b}\times\overrightarrow{c}+2\overrightarrow{c}\times\overrightarrow{a}+3\overrightarrow{a}\times \overrightarrow{b} \]

    where

    \[ l=1,m=2 \]

    and

    \[ n=3 \]
