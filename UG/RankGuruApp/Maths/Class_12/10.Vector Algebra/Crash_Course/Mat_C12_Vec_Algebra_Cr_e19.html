If three or more points lie on the same straight line then the points are collinear.



    Since, points lie on the same straight line, the area covered by them is zero.



    Three points make a triangle. Therefore, the area of the triangle is zero.



    We know that area of the triangle is



    \[ \dfrac 12(x_1(y_2-y_3)+x_2(y_3- y_1)\]


    \[+x_3(y_1-y_2)) \]

    \[ (a,-1),(2,1),(4,5) \]


    \[ \implies \dfrac 12(x_1(y_2-y_3)+x_2(y_3-y_1)\]


    \[+x_3(y_1-y_2))=0 \]


    \[ \implies a(1-5)+2(5-(-1))\]


    \[+4(-1-1)=0 \]
