Case I : In air


    Fundamental frequency,

    \[ f = \dfrac{1}{2L}\sqrt{\dfrac{T}{M}} \]


    \[ = \dfrac{1}{2L}\sqrt{\dfrac{p Vg}{M}} \]


    Where,

    \[ T\rightarrow \]

    Tension in air


    \[ V \rightarrow \]

    Volume


    \[ p \rightarrow \]

    Specific gravity


    \[ g \rightarrow \]

    Gravitational acceleration


    \[ M \rightarrow \]

    Linear density


    Case II: Immersed in water to half of the volume


    New Tension,

    \[ T' = T - \text{upthrust force} \]


    \[ \Rightarrow T' = T - \dfrac{V}{2}\rho g \]


    \[ \Rightarrow T' = Vpg - \dfrac{V}{2}g \]

        

    \[ [\rho_{water} = 1\space g/cc] \]


    \[ \Rightarrow T' = Vg(p - \dfrac{1}{2}) \]


    New fundamental frequency,

    \[ f' = \dfrac{1}{2L}\sqrt{\dfrac{T'}{M}} \]


    \[ = \dfrac{1}{2L}\sqrt{\dfrac{Vg(p - \dfrac{1}{2})}{M}} \]


    \[ \therefore \dfrac{f'}{f} = \dfrac{\dfrac{1}{2L}\sqrt{\dfrac{Vg(p - \dfrac{1}{2})}{M}}}{\dfrac{1}{2L}\sqrt{\dfrac{Vpg}{M}}} \]


    \[ \Rightarrow \dfrac{f'}{300} = \dfrac{1}{2L}\sqrt{\dfrac{Vg(p - \dfrac{1}{2})}{M}}\times \sqrt{\dfrac{M}{Vpg}} \]


    \[ \Rightarrow f' = 300(\dfrac{p - \dfrac{1}{2}}{p}) \]


    \[ \Rightarrow f' = 300(\dfrac{2p - 1}{2p}) \]
