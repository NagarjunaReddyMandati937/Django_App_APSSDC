Let mass per unit length of the rod is

    \[ m \]


    And take a strip of length

    \[ dx \]

    at a distance

    \[ x \]


    from the pivoted end Tension in the rod is due to the centrifugal force in the part of the rod that is away from the point of interest


    So, for the point at a distance

    \[ \dfrac{L}{4} \]


    \[ T_1=\displaystyle\int_{L/4}^L{(mdx)w^2xdx} \]


    \[ =\dfrac{15}{32}mw^2L^2 \]


    For a point at

    \[ \dfrac{3L}{4} \]


    \[ T_2=\displaystyle\int_{3L/4}^L{(mdx)w^2xdx} \]


    \[ =\dfrac{7}{32}mw^2L^2 \]


    So,

    \[ T_1>T_2 \]
