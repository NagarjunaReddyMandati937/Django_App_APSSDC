Given,


    Initial velocity

    \[ =u=54km/h \]


    \[ =54 \times \dfrac{5}{18}=15m/s \]


    Now, Distance traveled by car during reaction time:


    \[ time=t=0.2sec \]


    \[ v=0m/s \]


    \[ deceleration =a= 6m/s^2 \]


    \[ Distance=Speed\times time \]


    \[ Distance=15\times0.2 \]


    \[ =3m \]


    Now, From third equation of motion:


    \[ v^2-u^2=2as \]


    \[ 0^2-15\times15=2\times6\times s \]


    \[ s=\dfrac{-15\times15}{-12} \]


    \[ s=\dfrac{225}{12} \]


    \[ s=18.75m \]


    So the total distance traveled by the driver after he seeds the need to put the brakes ON

    \[ 18.75+3=21.75m \]
