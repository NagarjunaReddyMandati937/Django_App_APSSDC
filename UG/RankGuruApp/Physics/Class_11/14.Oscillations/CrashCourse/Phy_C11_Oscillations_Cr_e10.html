Modulus of rigidity

    \[ \eta=\cfrac{F}{A\theta} \]


    Here,

    \[ A=L^2 \]


    and

    \[ \theta=\cfrac{x}{L} \]


    for small

    \[ \theta \]


    \[ \therefore \]

    Restoring force

    \[ =F=-\eta A\theta \]


    or acceleration,

    \[ a=\cfrac{F}{m}=\cfrac{\eta L}{M}x\quad equation (1) \]


    \[ \because a\propto (-x) \]


    \[ \therefore \]

    Time period,

    \[ T=2\pi\sqrt{\left|\cfrac{x}{a}\right|} \]


    \[ \implies T=2\pi\sqrt {\cfrac{M}{\eta L}} \]
