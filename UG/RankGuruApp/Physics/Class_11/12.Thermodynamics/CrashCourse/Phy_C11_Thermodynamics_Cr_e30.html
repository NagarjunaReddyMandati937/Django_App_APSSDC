Heat absorbed by gas in three processes is given by


    \[ Q_{A C B}=\Delta U+W_{A C B} \]


    \[ Q_{A D B}=\Delta U \\ \]


    \[ Q_{A E B}=\Delta U+W_{A E B} \]


    The change in internal energy in all the three cases is same and

    \[ W_{A C B} \]

    is positive,

    \[ W_{A E B} \]

    is negative.


    Hence

    \[ \quad Q_{A C B}>Q_{A D B}>Q_{A E B} .\]
