Let

    \[ v \]

    be the velocity of the ball just before striking the surface of lift.


    Now,

    \[ a=g \]

    or

    \[ v^2= u^2+2as \]


    And

    \[ u=0 \]

    or

    \[ v_1= \sqrt {2gh_1} \]


    For upwards rebound:- The velocity with which its rebound is given be,


    \[ v_2= \sqrt {2gh_2} \]


    Also,

    \[e\]

    : coefficient of restitution is given by,



    \[ e= \cfrac {v_1}{v_2}\Rightarrow \sqrt {\cfrac {2gh_1}{2gh_2}} \]


    \[ \Rightarrow h_1=e^2h_2 \]


    For a lift moving with constant velocity :

    \[ h_1= e^2h_2 \]


    Statement 1 is the correct answer.
