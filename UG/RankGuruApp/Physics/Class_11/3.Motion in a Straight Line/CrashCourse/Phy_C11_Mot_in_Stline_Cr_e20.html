Distance between house and market

    \[ d = 2.5\ km \]


    From House to market :
    Speed of the person

    \[ \ v_1 = 5 km/hr \]


    \[\therefore \]

    Time taken

    \[ t_1 = \dfrac{2.5}{5} hr \]

    \[ = 0.5\ hr \]

    \[ = 30\ min \]



    From market to house :
    Speed of the person

    \[ \ v_2 = 7.5 km/hr \]


    \[\therefore \]

    Time taken

    \[ t_2 = \dfrac{2.5}{7.5} hr = \dfrac{1}{3} hr = 20 min\]



    Total distance covered

    \[ D = 2.5 + 2.5 =5km = 5000 m \]


    Total time taken

    \[ T = 30 + 20 = 50min \]

    \[ = 3000sec \]



    \[\therefore \]

    Average speed of the person

    \[ v_{avg} = \dfrac{5000}{3000} =\dfrac{5}{3}\ m/s \]
