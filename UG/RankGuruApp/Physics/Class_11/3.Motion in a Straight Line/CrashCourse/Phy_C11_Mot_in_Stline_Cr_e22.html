The displacement of the particle in

    \[ nth \]

    second

    \[ S_n = u+ \dfrac{1}{2}a (2n-1) \]


    Where all the symbols have their usual meaning.


    Given :

    \[ u = 0 \]


    \[ \implies S_n = \dfrac{1}{2} a(2n-1) \]


    Thus displacement in the

    \[ 3rd \]

    second

    \[ S_{3} = \dfrac{1}{2}a (2\times 3 - 1) =\dfrac{5a}{2} \]


    Displacement in the

    \[ 4th \]

    second

    \[ S_{4} = \dfrac{1}{2}a (2\times 4 - 1) =\dfrac{7a}{2} \]


    Percentage increase in the displacement

    \[ \dfrac{\Delta S}{S_3} \times 100 = \dfrac{S_4 - S_3}{S_3} \times 100 \]


    \[\dfrac{\Delta S}{S_3} \times 100 = \dfrac{\frac{7a}{2} -\frac{5a}{2}}{\frac{5a}{2}} \times 100 = 40\]


    Thus percentage increase in the displacement is

    \[ 40 \]
