For

    \[ 0-5 sec \]

    ,


    \[ a= 4m/s^2 \]

    , Let velocity at

    \[ 5 sec \]

    is

    \[ v \]

    and distance is

    \[ \ S_1 \]


    Using

    \[ v=u+at \]


    \[ \Rightarrow v= 0+4\times 5=20 m/s \]


    Using

    \[ s= ut+\dfrac{1}{2}at^2 \]


    \[ \Rightarrow S_1=0+\dfrac{1}{2}\times 4\times 5^2=50m \]


    For next

    \[ 25sec \]


    \[ v= 20 m/s \]


    \[ \Rightarrow S_2= 20\times 25= 500 m \]


    For comes to rest,


    \[ u= 20 m/s \]

    ,

    \[ a=-2 m/s^2 \]


    Using

    \[ v^2=u^2+2as \]


    \[ \Rightarrow 0=20^2-2\times 2\times S_3 \]


    \[ \Rightarrow S_3= 100 m \]


    Total distance

    \[ = S_1+S_2+S_3\]


    \[ = 50+500+100=650 m \]
