Given :

    \[ Q = 6.0\times 10^{-8} \ C \]


    Potential at distance

    \[ x = 3 \ m \]


    \[ V_1 = \dfrac{KQ}{x} = \dfrac{9\times 10^9\times 6\times 10^{-8}}{3} = 180 \ V \]


    Potential at distance

    \[ x = 6 \ m \]


    \[ V_2 = \dfrac{KQ}{x} = \dfrac{9\times 10^9\times 6\times 10^{-8}}{6} = 90 \ V \]


    Charge of electron

    \[ e = -1.6\times 10^{-19} \ C \]


    Work done

    \[ W = e(V_2-V_1) = -1.6\times 10^{-19}(90 - 180) \]


    \[ = 1.44\times 10^{-17} \ J \]
