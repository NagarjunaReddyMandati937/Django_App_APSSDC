When total internal reflection tales place from lateral surface,

    \[ i=C \]


    \[ \Rightarrow 60^{0}=C \]


    \[ \Rightarrow sinC=sin60^0=\dfrac{1}{\mu} \]


    \[ \Rightarrow \dfrac{1}{\mu}=\dfrac{\sqrt3}{2} \]


    \[ \Rightarrow \mu=\dfrac{2}{\sqrt3} \]


    Time taken by the light to travel some distance in medium

    \[ t=\dfrac{\mu x} {c} \]

    \[ \Rightarrow \dfrac{\mu x}{c}=\dfrac{\dfrac{2}{\sqrt3}\times 10^3}{3\times 10^8} \]

    \[ \Rightarrow \dfrac{\mu x}{c}=3.85\mu sec \]
