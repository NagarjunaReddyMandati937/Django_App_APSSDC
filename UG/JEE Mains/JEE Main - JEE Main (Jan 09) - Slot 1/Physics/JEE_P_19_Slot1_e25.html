\(\dfrac{hc}{\lambda_1} = \phi + \dfrac{1}{2} m (2v)^2\)



    \(\dfrac{hc}{\lambda_2} = \phi + \dfrac{1}{2} mv^2\)



    \(\Rightarrow \dfrac{\dfrac{hc}{\lambda_1}-\phi}{\dfrac{hc}{\lambda_2}-\phi} = 4\)



    \(\Rightarrow \dfrac{hc}{\lambda_1} - \phi = \dfrac{4hc}{\lambda_2} - 4\phi\)



    \(\Rightarrow \dfrac{4hc}{\lambda_2} - \dfrac{hc}{\lambda_1} = 3\phi\)



    \(\Rightarrow \phi = \dfrac{1}{3} hc \left(\dfrac{4}{\lambda_2} - \dfrac{1}{\lambda_1}\right)\)



    \(= \dfrac{1}{3} \times 1240 \left(\dfrac{4\times 350 - 540}{350\times 540}\right)\)



    \(=1.8eV\)
