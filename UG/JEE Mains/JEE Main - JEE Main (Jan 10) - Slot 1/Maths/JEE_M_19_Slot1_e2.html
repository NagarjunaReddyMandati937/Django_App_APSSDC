\(f(x)=x^3+x^2f'(1)+xf''(2)+f'''(3)\)



    \(\Rightarrow f'(x)=3x^2+2xf'(1)+f''(2)\).....\((1)\)



    \(\Rightarrow f''(x)=6x+2f'(1)\).........\((2)\)



    \(\Rightarrow f'''(x)=6\).................\((3)\)



    Put \(x=1\) in equation \((1)\) :



    \(f'(1)=3+2f'(1)+f''(2)\)...........\((4)\)



    Put \(x=2\) in equation \((2)\) :



    \(f''(2)=12+2f'(1)\)



    from equations \((4)\) & \((5)\)



    \(-3-f'(1)=12+2f'(1)\)



    \(\Rightarrow 3f'(1)=-15\)



    \(\Rightarrow f'(1)=-5\)



    \(\Rightarrow f''(2)=2\)



    Put \(x=3\) in equation \((3)\) :



    \(f'''(3)=6\)
