\(\displaystyle\sum^{20}_{i=1}\left(\dfrac{^{20}C_{i-1}}{^{20}C_i+{^{20}C_{i-1}}}\right)^3=\dfrac{k}{21}\)



    \(\Rightarrow \displaystyle\sum^{20}_{i=1}\left(\dfrac{^{20}C_{i-1}}{^{21}C_i}\right)^3=\dfrac{k}{21}\)



    \(\Rightarrow \displaystyle\sum^{20}_{i=1}\left(\dfrac{i}{21}\right)^3=\dfrac{k}{21}\)



    \(\Rightarrow \dfrac{1}{(21)^3}\left[\dfrac{20(21)}{2}\right]^2=\dfrac{k}{21}\)



    \(\Rightarrow 100=k\)
